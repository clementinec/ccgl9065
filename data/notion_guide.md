# Notion Setup Guide

Your Notion portfolio is where you document your weekly research and reflections throughout the semester. Follow these steps to get started.

---

## Step 1: Duplicate the Template

1. Open the [CCGL9065 Portfolio Template](https://petite-impatiens-e81.notion.site/2e52932a4d0a800fb84ce93ee974aa0b?v=2e52932a4d0a80ef90ad000cd9030046)
2. Click the **"Duplicate"** button in the top-right corner
3. Select your personal workspace when prompted
4. The template will appear in your Notion sidebar

**Can't find the Duplicate button?** See [Notion's official guide](https://www.notion.com/help/duplicate-public-pages).

---

## Step 2: Make It Yours

Once duplicated:

- Rename the page with your name (e.g., "Jane's CCGL9065 Portfolio")
- Add your weekly entries following the format in the template
- Keep everything organized by week

---

## Step 3: Publish Your Page

You need to publish your page so you can share links in Slack.

1. Open your portfolio page in Notion
2. Click **"Share"** in the top-right corner
3. Go to the **"Publish"** tab
4. Click **"Publish"**
5. Copy the public URL

**Need more details?** See [Notion's publishing guide](https://www.notion.com/help/public-pages-and-web-publishing).

---

## Step 4: Share in Slack

When posting to the weekly Slack channel:

1. Include your Spectacle (Fact + Story + Stakes)
2. Add the link to your Notion page at the end: `ðŸ”— Full research: [your-notion-url]`
